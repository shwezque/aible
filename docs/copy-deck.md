## Tone Guidelines

**Do:**
- Talk like a smart friend who's excited about AI
- Use "you" and "your" — it's personal
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature
- Celebrate progress generously
- Make wrong answers feel like learning, not failure

**Don't:**
- Use jargon (LLM, NLP, fine-tuning, tokens, parameters)
- Say "simple" or "easy" — let the experience prove it
- Be condescending ("Don't worry, anyone can do this!")
- Use exclamation points more than once per screen
- Write paragraphs when a sentence will do
- Reference specific AI tool UIs ("Click the gear icon in ChatGPT")
