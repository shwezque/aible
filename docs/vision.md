# Vision

Aible exists because millions of people know AI matters but have no idea how to actually use it. They've poked around ChatGPT, watched a few YouTube videos, and still can't point to a single result. The gap between awareness and ability is massive — and nothing on the market closes it the way Duolingo closed the gap for languages.

Aible is Duolingo for AI. It takes non-technical people from "I've heard of AI" to "I use AI every day to work faster and earn more" — through gamified, bite-sized daily lessons that adapt to where you are and where you want to go. The experience is fun, addictive, and progressively challenging. You earn XP, maintain streaks, unlock new skills, and level up through a structured curriculum that covers everything from prompting basics to building AI-powered income streams.

**Be AI-able.**
